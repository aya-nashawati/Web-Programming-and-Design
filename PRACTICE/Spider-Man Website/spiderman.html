Who is Spider-Man?

Bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter Parker’s arachnid abilities give him amazing powers that he uses to help others.

With amazing spider-like abilities, this teenage science whiz fights crime and dreams of becoming an Avenger as Spider-Man.

He was created by writer-editor Stan Lee and writer-artist Steve Ditko.

He first appeared in the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 in the Silver Age of Comic Books.
