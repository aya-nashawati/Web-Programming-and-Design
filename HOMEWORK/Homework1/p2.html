About

Founded in 1866, the American University of Beirut bases its educational philosophy, standards, and practices on the American liberal arts model of higher education. A teaching-centered research university, AUB has around 800 Instructional faculty and a student body pf around 8,000 students. The University encourages freedom of thought and expression and seeks to graduate men and women committed to creative and critical thinking, life-long learning, personal intergity, civic responsivility, and leadership. AUB's motto is That They May Have Life And Have It More Abundantly.
